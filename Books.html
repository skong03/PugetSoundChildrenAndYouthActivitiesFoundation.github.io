Our Book Wish List is an initiative to provide engaging reading materials to young readers. Below are key guidelines and considerations for participating in this program:

  • Submissions are not guaranteed to be fulfilled due to limited resources.
  • Book availability depends on our current funding and donations.
  • Books are chosen based on age suitability, educational value, and availability.
  • Wish list status will be updated on our website and communicated via email.
  • Consider supporting us with a donation to help fulfill more wishes.
  • We're grateful for your support and strive to enrich young readers' experiences.
