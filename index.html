Our Mission

    The Puget Sound Children and Youth Activities Foundation dedicates itself to enhancing the lives of young people in the Puget Sound region through a variety of activities and programs. Our aim is to support comprehensive development encompassing basketball, swimming, chess, Rubik's Cube, badminton, among other activities. In addition to our regular offerings, we organize special events and camps that delve into specific interests while fostering skills in leadership and teamwork.
